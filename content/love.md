---
title: Real Love
---

My [[life]] has left me  
for love of the Real.  
Just one letter remains.  
  
No state. No station.  
Nothing higher.  
Nothing lower.  
Nothing other  
than this,  
for its own sake.  
  
Feel it and find there is  
no feeling.  
  
I am that  
empty field,  
[[soul]]ful.
