---
title: Heartful
---

Still a foolish scholar,  
still a naked man  
bare before the gaze,  
now gifted simplicity.  
  
None of this matters:  
reality is nothing   
like a poem.  
  
It's only placed here  
to perfect one [[turn]]  
of threaded truth:  
  
empty hands,   
[[heart]] overflowing.
